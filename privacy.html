Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 11, 2025

1. Introduction

Emergency Lifeline ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website Emergency Lifeline (the "Service"). Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access the Service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Data

We may collect personally identifiable information ("Personal Data") that you voluntarily provide to us when you register for the Service, create a profile, use certain features, or contact us. This may include:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number.
  • Profile Information: Age, gender, blood type, medical conditions, allergies, emergency contacts.
  • Location Data: Your precise or approximate location when you enable location services for emergency alerts.
  • Communication Data: Information you provide when you communicate with us (e.g., customer support inquiries).

2.2 Non-Personal Data

We may also collect non-personal information, which cannot be used to identify you, such as:

  • Usage Data (e.g., pages visited, features used, time spent on the Service).
  • Device Information (e.g., device type, operating system, browser type).
  • Aggregated data (e.g., statistics about user demographics or feature usage).

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

  • To provide and maintain the Service, including managing your profile and emergency contacts.
  • To send emergency alerts to your designated contacts with your location, if enabled by you.
  • To personalize your experience on the Service and deliver content and features relevant to your needs.
  • To improve the Service, diagnose technical problems, and conduct research and analysis.
  • To communicate with you, including responding to your inquiries and providing customer support.
  • To send you updates, health alerts, and other notifications relevant to the Service (with your consent where required).
  • To enforce our Terms of Service and other policies.
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect our rights.

4. How We Share Your Information

We may share your information in the following situations:

  • With Your Consent: We may disclose your personal information if you give us specific permission to do so. For example, your location and profile information are shared with your emergency contacts ONLY when you trigger an alert.
  • Emergency Contacts: Information specifically designated by you (e.g., your location, medical details) will be shared with your listed emergency contacts during a crisis or alert, as initiated by you.
  • Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf (e.g., hosting, analytics, customer support). These providers are obligated to protect your information and use it only for the purposes for which it was disclosed.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court order or government agency).
  • Business Transfers: In connection with, or during negotiations of, any merger, sale of company assets, financing, or acquisition of all or a portion of our business to another company.
  • For Protection of Rights: To protect the rights, property, or safety of Emergency Lifeline, our users, or the public, as required or permitted by law.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical, administrative, and physical security measures designed to protect your Personal Data from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. Therefore, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

6.1 Account Information

You may review, update, correct, or delete the information in your account at any time by logging into your profile settings. If you wish to delete your account, please contact us.

6.3 Communications

Our Service is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone under the age of 13.
